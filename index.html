IntelliCom Lab

IntellliCom Lab, as the name states focuses on next-generation research towards intellligent communication and computations. Broadly, we work on the areas of Federated Learning, Explainable AI, Causal Inference and Survival Analysis. Our application areas are vast including IoTs, healthcare, retail, etc. Our core mathematical areas are Probabilistic Machine Learning, Distributed Learning and Statistical Inference
